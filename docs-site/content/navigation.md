### Using Search
- Press **Cmd/Ctrl + K** to open search
- Search by topic, concept, or company
- Searches include code examples

- Click sections to expand/collapse
- Articles organized by topic
- Difficulty: 🟢 Beginner, 🟡 Intermediate, 🔴 Advanced

- Right sidebar shows article sections
- Click to jump to specific section
- Auto-highlights current section

- 🟡 **Intermediate** - Requires basic knowledge
